Bluesky Directory and Flipboard Partner on Custom Feeds in Surf Social Browser

Posted on July 23, 2025 in Product, Bluesky Directory by John Luther

We've teamed up with Flipboard to make custom feeds created in Surf social browser discoverable in Bluesky Directory! Read the full announcement on the Flipboard blog.

Surf creates a personalized social experience tailored to your interests, not the "For You" algorithms of closed social platforms. Get the Surf beta at surf.social with code SURF+DIRECTORY, and then explore Surf feeds at Bluesky Directory.
